MAAS documentation

MAAS is Metal As A Service, a service that lets you treat physical servers like virtual machines – instances – in the cloud. No need for you to manage servers individually: MAAS turns your bare metal into an elastic, cloud-like resource.

Quick questions you might have:

  • What is MAAS – and what does it really do for me?
  • Can you show me an example data centre using MAAS?
  • How does MAAS work – in detail?
  • What concepts might I need to understand before starting?
  • Can I just install it and try it for myself?
